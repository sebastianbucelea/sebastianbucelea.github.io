Overview

Thousands of years have passed since the glorious days of the Aztec Empire. The recipient containing the heart of the well-known hero Achcauhtli came to life when the civilisation has disappeared. The heart of the hero is now not only looking for its body but, but for freedom as well. The Island of Cechua is now a prison for the soul of the mighty warrior. Will you be able to free it from the remote pile of earth and sand?
